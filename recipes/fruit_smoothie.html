Sweet, nutritious, delicious!

I don't know about you, but I love smoothies! Smoothies are a great way to get a ton of nutrients and essentials for our body to function efficiently. You can even add vegetables to get things like iron and vitamin K, but we're not doing that in this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen fruit
  • 1 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 2 scoops protein powder
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine frozen fruit, greek yogurt, protein powder, and milk together.
  2. Blend until smooth, and enjoy!
