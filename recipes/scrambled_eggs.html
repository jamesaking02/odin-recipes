Scrambled Eggs


Fluffy, tasty, and healthy!

It's a perfect morning, maybe you just woke up, or you got up early to get some exercise in to start the day. Eggs are a great source of good cholestoral and protein, they're good for after an intense workout to replenish those muscles. Many people don't know the correct way to make eggs, but don't worry, because here's the secret recipe! This is a recipe on how to make the perfect scrambled eggs. So, get your breakfast started right with some scrambled eggs!

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 Tablespoons of butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs until thoroughly scrambled.
  2. Chop up the butter into small cubes and mix it into the eggs.
  3. Butter a saute pan and heat to medium/high.
  4. Add the eggs to the pan and let it cook for about a minute. Stir the eggs with a wooden spoon until thoroughly cooked.
  5. Add salt and pepper for flavor!

    6. Enjoy! :)
